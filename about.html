Open Ethiopia

a simple open data driven application

About The Project

This project was done to complemet a Master's Thesis in the Czech University of Life Sciences Prague on the topic of "Open Data for Ethiopia". A partial objective of the Thesis was to demonstrate the use of Open Data driven application aimed at facilitating the development of the Ethiopia in meeting one of the Sustainable Development Goals, i.e Zero Hunger by the year 2030.

Methodology

A Data API provided by the An International Food Policy Research Institute together with Food security indicators dataset made available by Food and Agricultural organization of the United Nations in December, 2016 was used to contract the data visualizations available on this website.

Future Works

The author of this Thesis plans to, moving forward, include additional resources to make data and data visualizations readily available to users. This additional resources will also include data visualization requests made by the users of the website.
